Clash of Clans Characters
Heroes
* Barbarian King — Massive melee leader wielding giant sword.
* Archer Queen — Precise ranged hero with stealth ability.
* Grand Warden — Support mage boosting nearby troops.
* Royal Champion — Agile spear warrior targeting defenses.
* Battle Machine — Builder Base mechanical smashing hero.
* Battle Copter — Flying mechanical hero with ranged rockets.

* Wall Breaker — Skeleton bomber specialized in destroying walls.
* Balloon — Air unit dropping powerful bombs.
* Wizard — Glass-cannon mage launching explosive fireballs.
* Healer — Flying support unit restoring ally health.
* Dragon — Durable flying attacker breathing destructive flames.
* P.E.K.K.A — Heavy armored warrior delivering devastating melee strikes.
* Baby Dragon — Small enraged dragon gaining solo damage bonus.
* Miner — Underground troop surfacing directly beside targets.
* Electro Dragon — Lightning dragon chaining electrical attacks between targets.
* Yeti — Icy beast spawning damaging mini creatures.
* Dragon Rider — Rider mounted on airborne dragon attacker.
* Electro Titan — Towering warrior emitting damaging electrical aura.
* Root Rider — Cavalry smashing through walls while charging forward.
* Thrower — Long-range siege troop launching heavy projectiles.
